Drop Shipping Online App

Summary

Drop Shipping Online App responds to common offline shopping problems such as difficulty finding authentic shops, long travel distances, limited product details, and time-consuming manual product inquiries. The system was designed to move these interactions into an online workflow with clearer browsing, registration, package selection, booking, and management features.

The application supports user registration and login, searchable event or service categories with auto-suggestions, package browsing, date and time selection, booking receipt generation, and communication between clients and managers. Admin users can view and update customer records, pending orders, product details, payment transactions, order status, feedback, remarks, and reported issues.

The system was built with Django and SQLite on the backend, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the frontend, and payment API integration. Its initial scope is Bangladesh, with early service availability focused on Dhaka and future plans for broader coverage.
