WhyMedQA: Enhanced Biomedical Why Question Answering

Summary

WhyMedQA is a specialized biomedical question-answering system designed to provide precise, context-aware medical answers. The work addresses the limitations of general-purpose language models in medical settings, where specialized vocabulary and strict accuracy requirements make unreliable responses especially risky.

The model uses a transfer learning approach built on the pre-trained BART transformer architecture. Domain-specific enhancements were added to the decoder output, including a custom multi-head attention layer, dense projection layer, and dropout regularization.

WhyMedQA was fine-tuned on the BioASQ8 challenge dataset and an expert-annotated subset of PubMedQA. It was evaluated against baseline models such as GPT-2, T5, and BioGPT using BLEU and ROUGE metrics, achieving stronger answer relevance, reduced training and validation losses, and effective performance with only 142 million parameters.

Its lightweight design makes it suitable for resource-constrained healthcare environments, including low-capacity health applications and rural clinics. By delivering reliable medical information quickly, WhyMedQA aims to support evidence-based medicine, reduce decision-making errors, and improve patient outcomes.
